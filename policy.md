AI Processing and Third-Party Services

Forma Max uses artificial intelligence services to analyze meal photos and generate nutrition estimates.

When a user scans a meal using the camera or uploads a food image, the following data may be transmitted securely to third-party AI processing services:

• Food photos captured by the user
• Food descriptions entered by the user
• Optional nutrition context related to the meal

This data is used solely to generate calorie and nutrition estimates for the user.

Forma Max does not transmit personally identifying information such as name, email address, or account credentials to AI processing services as part of meal analysis.

Third-party AI services process this data only for the purpose of generating nutrition analysis and are required to maintain privacy and security protections equal to or greater than those required by our platform.

User Consent

Before any meal photo or food description is sent for AI analysis, Forma Max requests the user's explicit permission to process the data using third-party AI services. Users may decline AI analysis and still use other features of the app.

Data Usage

Meal images and food descriptions are used for the following purposes:

• Generating calorie and macronutrient estimates
• Providing nutrition insights to the user
• Improving food recognition accuracy

Data Retention

Food images may be temporarily processed by AI services but are not permanently stored by Forma Max unless the user saves the meal in their nutrition log.

Users may delete meal entries at any time from within the app.

# Forma Max – Data Deletion

Forma Max allows users to delete specific data, such as meals and nutrition logs, without deleting their account.

## Delete data in the app

1. Open Forma Max
2. Navigate to your meal or nutrition log
3. Select the item you want to delete
4. Tap the delete option

## Request data deletion manually

If you are unable to access the app, you can request data deletion by emailing:

**[support@formamax.ai](mailto:support@formamax.ai)**

Include:

* The email associated with your account
* A description of the data you want deleted (e.g., meals, logs)

## What data can be deleted

* Meals and nutrition logs
* User-generated tracking data

## What data may be retained

* Account information (unless account deletion is requested)
* Data required for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes

## Data retention

* Deleted data is removed immediately or within a short processing period
* Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for system integrity

If you have any questions, contact **[support@formamax.ai](mailto:support@formamax.ai)**

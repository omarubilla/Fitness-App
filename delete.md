# Forma Max – Account & Data Deletion

Forma Max allows users to delete their account directly داخل the app or by request.

## Delete your account in the app

1. Open Forma Max
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Account Details**
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Confirm deletion

## Request deletion manually

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing:

**[support@formamax.ai](mailto:kevin@backfield.ai)**

Include:

* The email associated with your account
* Subject line: "Delete My Account"

## What data is deleted

* Account information (email and profile data)
* Nutrition and fitness tracking data
* App usage data associated with your account

## Data retention

* Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for security and fraud prevention
* After this period, data is permanently deleted

## Important

* Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
* Active subscriptions must be managed through Google Play

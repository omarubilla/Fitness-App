# Forma Max – Account & Data Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your Forma Max account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

### How to request deletion

1. Send an email to: **[support@formamax.ai](mailto:support@formamax.ai)**
2. Use the subject line: **"Delete My Account"**
3. Include the email address associated with your account

### What data is deleted

* Account information (email and profile data)
* Nutrition and fitness tracking data
* App usage data associated with your account

### Data retention

* Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for security and fraud prevention purposes
* After this period, all data is permanently deleted

### Additional notes

* Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone
* You will lose access to any active subscriptions

If you have any questions, contact **[support@formamax.ai](mailto:support@formamax.ai)**
